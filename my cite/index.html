About me

I am expert in developing concepts, graphics and layouts for product illustrations, company logos and websites. determining size and arrangement of copy and illustrative material, as well as font style and size. preparing rough drafts of material based on an agreed brief.

I can also create visual concepts to communicate information. Create everything from posters and billboards to packaging, logos and marketing materials. We use elements such as shapes, colors, typography, images and more to convey ideas to an audience.

My strength is Precision, attention to detail, passion for my work, which reflects in each painting or drawing. These are my greatest strengths as a graphic designer.
